The Mindpal Methodology

Our clinically validated approach combines technology with therapeutic best practices for measurable results.

1

Comprehensive Assessment

Begin with a detailed psychological evaluation and needs assessment conducted by our AI system and verified by licensed clinicians to create your personalized therapy profile.

2

Customized Program Design

Our clinical team develops a tailored VR therapy protocol combining multiple evidence-based modalities to address your specific mental health goals and challenges.

3

Immersive Therapy Sessions

Engage in guided VR experiences that adapt in real-time to your physiological responses, with AI-assisted therapeutic guidance and clinician oversight.

4

Progress Tracking & Adjustment

Continuous data collection and analysis allow for dynamic program adjustments, with weekly progress reviews by your assigned mental health professional.

5

Sustainable Wellness

Gradual transition to maintenance program with tools and techniques for long-term mental health management and relapse prevention strategies.
